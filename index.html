FAQ

How are donations handled?

Funds accumulate in a multisig wallet managed by community signers. Periodically, signers execute transfers to registered organizations and publish TX links here and on X.

What about the LP?

pump.fun uses a bonding curve; once completed, liquidity is migrated automatically to a DEX. Always confirm details on your token page.

Do you use real-person images?

No, unless there's explicit consent. We use symbolic imagery (candles, ribbons, roads) to respect privacy and dignity.

Risks?

Crypto is risky. No guarantees, no refunds. Spend only what you can afford to lose. Verify all addresses from official links.
