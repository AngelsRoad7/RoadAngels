FAQ

How are donations handled?

Funds accumulate in a multisig wallet managed by signers from the community. Periodically, signers execute transfers to registered organizations and publish TX links here and on X.

What about the LP?

pump.fun uses a bonding curve; once the token "completes" per pump.fun criteria, it migrates liquidity to a DEX automatically. Always confirm details on your token page.

Any real‑person imagery?

We avoid using photos of real victims without explicit consent. We use symbolic art (candles, ribbons) to respect privacy and dignity.

Risks?

Crypto is risky. Taxes and parameters are visible on pump.fun; there are no guarantees or refunds. Always verify addresses before sending.
